Instructions
Registration process (we recommend you to use a laptop/pc)

1) Enter your name, email address, and phone number
2) Select the type of ticket you wish to purchase, then select the number of tickets that you require
3) Scan the QR code to pay the amount mentioned.
(If using a smartphone, take a screenshot of the QR code and scan it through your UPI app)
4) Once paid, enter the transaction ID in the registration form and click "submit"
